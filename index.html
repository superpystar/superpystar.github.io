About Me

Welcome to my academic website. I am a PhD student at RWTH Aachen University with research interests in Fluid Mechanics and Computational Fluid Dynamics. My work focuses on understanding complex fluid phenomena, including fluid instabilities and multiphase flow, using advanced numerical simulation methods. Currently, I am working on bubble-laden turbulent flows using the Lattice Boltzmann Method (LBM) coupled with the Level Set method.
